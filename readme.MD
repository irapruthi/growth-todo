🌿 ZenGarden: Deep Work Ecosystem

ZenGarden is a gamified productivity tool that transforms your focus time into a living, breathing digital garden. Unlike traditional to-do lists, every task you plant starts as a seed in a vast soil grid. As you complete your deep work sessions, your background evolves from barren earth into a lush landscape of flowers, fruits, and exotic plants.

 Key Features:

🌱 Interactive Planting: Adding a task "plants" a seed in a specific plot on your background grid.

 Evolutionary Timer: Watch your seed grow through 7 stages—from a New Moon 🌑 to a Full Tree 🌳—in real-time as the timer counts down.

🍎 Diverse Reward Pool: Complete tasks to grow a variety of over 50+ icons, including Watermelons, Persimmons, Lotuses, and rare Gemstones.

 Dynamic Liquid UI: Features a glassmorphism control panel with a "liquid wave" that accelerates as your timer nears completion.

 Zen Soundscape: Built-in forest ambience and bird chirps to help maintain deep focus (with mute toggle).

 Title System: Earn titles like Sprout Scout and Nature Deity based on your lifetime focus hours.

Persistent Forest: Your garden is saved locally in your browser—your progress is never lost, even if you refresh or close the tab.

 Built With

HTML5 & CSS3: For the glassmorphism UI and the CSS Sine-Wave animations.

Vanilla JavaScript: To handle the grid logic, timer calculations, and localStorage persistence.
HOW TO USE:

1.Plant: Enter a task in the input field and click Plant Seed. A 🌱 will appear in a random soil plot on your background.

2.Focus: Click the task in your list. Use the slider to choose your focus duration (from 30 seconds to 2 hours).

3.Grow: Click Start Growth. The Zen audio will begin, and the tree in the modal will evolve.

4.Harvest: Once the timer hits 00:00:00, the task is removed from your list, and the seed on the background permanently transforms into a beautiful plant!
